**How to get your KASHER onto a BIC® or CLIPPER® Lighter, and how to keep it tight:**

# Classic KASHER (made to fit a Bic) Tips: 

# ![How to place a Kasher lighter tool on a Bic Lighter. Slide the Kasher on your lighter by holding the lighter vertically, tilting the Kasher at a 45% angle with the Kasher stem near the lighter's ignition hole, and push/slide the Kasher down. If the Kasher needs to be tightened, simply pinch the flaps that hold the lighter's body.](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0264/9184/6755/files/Put-a-kasher-on-a-bic_480x480.jpg?v=1608075339)

Simply place the KASHER on the top of the Bic lighter at a 45º angle, and press it down over the metal head. From there, it should snap down to easily slide down your lighter.

![How to tighten a Kasher for the Bic Style Lighter.](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0264/9184/6755/files/Tighten-Kasher-for-bic_480x480.jpg?v=1608075338)

If the standard KASHER ever becomes loose on your BIC lighter and needs to be tightened, simply pinch the flaps that hold the lighter's body together until you reach the desired tightness.

# 360 KASHER (made to fit a Clipper) Tips:

![Slide the Kasher on your Clipper lighter starting from the bottom side of the lighter. With Kasher and Clipper word marks on the same side and alignment, slide the Kasher up to rest on the lighter's plastic body. ](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0264/9184/6755/files/Put-a-kasher-on-a-clipper_480x480.jpg?v=1608075338)

Slide the KASHER on your CLIPPER lighter starting from the bottom side of the lighter. With the KASHER and CLIPPER word marks on the same side and alignment, slide the KASHER upward to rest on the lighter's body. 

![If your Kasher 360 becomes loose on your Clipper lighter, pinch the side flaps \(which hold the lighter's body\) towards one another. ](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0264/9184/6755/files/Tighten-Kasher-for-clipper_480x480.jpg?v=1608075338)

If your KASHER 360 becomes loose on your CLIPPER lighter, pinch the side flaps (which hold the lighter's body) towards one another.

# How to fix the tip sticking out & snagging on clothing:

![Kasher sticking out away from Lighter crossed out](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0264/9184/6755/files/KasherUseIcons_stickingout_KasherPlus-VapePen-copy-9_480x480.jpg?v=1608252042)

![Arrows indicating where to adjust the Kasher for the best fit](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0264/9184/6755/files/KasherUseIcons_stickingout_KasherPlus-VapePen-copy-10_480x480.jpg?v=1608252071)

Take your KASHER off of the lighter, and slightly pinch the bottom flaps that hold the lighter's body, and gently bend the tip towards the body of the lighter (make sure to do this while the KASHER is off your lighter). Adjust to your desired tightness.
