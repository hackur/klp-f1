**_What’s The Deal With Titanium?_**

The Kasher TITANIUM® lighter tool is not made with an alloy or with titanium chemical coating on steel. It’s commercially pure grade 2 titanium, which we use for its high corrosion resistance and strength to weight ratio.

There are two types of titanium — pure and alloy. Both are used in lots of industries, including: aerospace, military, medical, jewelry, telecommunications and more. Commercially pure titanium is over 99% pure while titanium alloy (also called “memory titanium” or “bendable titanium,”) is about 75% pure with 25% of other metals such as nickel or copper added. Both kinds are lightweight, durable and strong, but pure titanium exceeds titanium alloys in _all_ of these qualities.
